Habitat destruction, climate change and pollution are major threats to biodiversity, causing irreversible damage to ecosystems and species extinction.
Human activities like deforestation, overfishing and invasive species introduction exacerbate these threats. If left unchecked, biodiversity loss will compromise ecosystem services, human well-being and global food security.
